Visualization Three: City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Very little correlation can be observed between city latitude and average cloudiness. In this instance, cloudiness is defined as the average percentage of cloud cover observed on any given day. As this value is prone to producing rougher estimates, our cloudiness percentage number is rounded, leading to data arranged in horizontal rows.
