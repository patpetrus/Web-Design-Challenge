Visualization Two: City Latitude vs. Humidity

This visualization highlights how little the latitude of an area and its accompanying levels of humidity correspond. No trends can be observed. Outliers at the bottom right end of the graph represent cities from the northernmost parts of the world, where the air is often dry and frigid.
